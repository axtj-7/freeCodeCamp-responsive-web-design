Lasagna de carnivale Recipe

Lasagna de Carnivale is a joyful, festive dish made to celebrate togetherness, indulgence, and tradition. Rich layers of pasta, slow-cooked meat sauce, and creamy cheeses come together to create a hearty lasagna that is perfect for special occasions and cheerful family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets – 12 sheets
  • Minced meat (beef or beef + pork) – 500 g
  • Onion (finely chopped) – 1 medium (100 g)
  • Garlic (minced) – 4 cloves
  • Tomato puree / crushed tomatoes – 500 g
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Cheese – 500 g
  • Egg – 1 large
  • Salt – 1½ teaspoons (or to taste)
  • Black pepper – 1 teaspoon
  • Dried oregano or basil – 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil; sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add minced meat and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato puree, salt, pepper, and herbs; simmer until thick.
  4. Mix ricotta, parmesan, egg (optional), salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Spread meat sauce in a baking dish and place lasagna sheets on top.
  6. Add ricotta mixture and mozzarella; repeat layers as needed.
  7. Finish with meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35–40 minutes, rest 10 minutes, then serve.
